 Urgent: Ramos accepts Duterte's offer to be special envoy to China: media



MANILA, July 23 (Xinhua) -- Former Philippine President Fidel
Ramos told reporters in Davao on Saturday evening that he accepted
the offer of President Rogrigo Duterte to be special envoy to China
after their two-hour meeting in Davao, media reports said on
Saturday. Enditem
