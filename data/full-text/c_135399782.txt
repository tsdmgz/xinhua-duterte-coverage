 Philippine Congress proclaims Duterte as presidential winner



QUEZON CITY, May 30, 2016 (Xinhua) -- Philippine vice presidential winner Leni Robredo (C) is congratulated by congressmen during the proclamation of president and vice president at the House of Representatives in Quezon City, the Philippines, May 30, 2016. Philippine Congress proclaimed Rodrigo Duterte as presidential winner and Leni Robredo as vice presidential winner on Monday. (Xinhua/Rouelle Umali)
