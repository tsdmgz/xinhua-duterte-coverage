 Major news items in leading German newspapers



BERLIN, Oct. 12 (Xinhua) -- The following are the major news
items in leading German newspapers on Wednesday.

Sueddeutsche Zeitung:
-- After batteries and replacement devices repeatedly caught on
fire, Samsung decided on Tuesday to take its smartphone Galaxy Note
7 recently presented in August completely from the market.

Die Welt:
-- Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte now wants to take action
against smoking besides his fight against illegal drug
consumption.

Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung:
-- German Chancellor Angela Merkel has called on the countries
of Africa to strengthen their cooperation in the fight against
flight, terrorism and economic problems. Enditem
