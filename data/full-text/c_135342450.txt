 90-day election campaign period in Philippines ends



MANILA, May 8, 2016 (Xinhua) -- Philippine Presidential candidate and Davao City Mayor Rodrigo Duterte speaks to his supporters during a grand campaign rally in Manila, capital of the Philippines, on May 7, 2016. The 90-day campaign period for the national candidates in the Philippines May 9 elections ended on Saturday. (Xinhua/Rouelle Umali)
