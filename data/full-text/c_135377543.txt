 Cake made to celebrate Rodrigo Duterte's victory during election




QUEZON CITY, May 21, 2016 (Xinhua) -- Photo taken on May 21, 2016 shows a cake featuring presumptive Philippine president-elect Rodrigo Duterte at a bakeshop in Quezon City, the Philippines. The cake is made to celebrate a large portion of almost 16 million votes Rodrigo Duterte won during the Philippine presidential election. (Xinhua/Rouelle Umali)
