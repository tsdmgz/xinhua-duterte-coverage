90-day campaign period for Philippine national election starts

Source: Xinhua 2016-02-10 07:20:00

ILOCOS NORTE PROVINCE, Feb. 9, 2016 (Xinhua) -- Presidential candidate Senator Miriam Defesor-Santiago (R) join hands with her running mate Senator Ferdinand Marcos Jr. during their campaign in Ilocos Norte Province, the Philippines, Feb. 9, 2016. The 90-day campaign period for the national candidates in the Philippine May elections officially starts Tuesday.(Xinhua/Stringer)
